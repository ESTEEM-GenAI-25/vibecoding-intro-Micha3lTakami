Hip-hop collective, 2015–2022

About

BROCKHAMPTON is a creative collective formed in Texas and based in Los Angeles. Known for their genre-blending sound, colorful visuals, and a DIY ethos, they released several critically acclaimed albums between 2017 and 2022.

Discography

SATURATION Trilogy (2017)
Three albums in one year that defined their early sound.
IRIDESCENCE (2018)
Debuted #1 on Billboard 200 with bold experimentation.
GINGER (2019)
Softer, reflective palette with hits like “SUGAR.”
ROADRUNNER (2021)
Big collabs, hope-filled anthems.
TM (2022)
Final release, a heartfelt goodbye.
